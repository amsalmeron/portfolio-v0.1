Waste No More


Waste No More is an application that allows users to sign, input items from their fridge along with an expiration date and return suggested recipes. The recipe finder will prioritize soon to be expired food to make sure no food goes to waste. The goal of this application is to bring awareness to food waste and help others become more concious to what is in their fridge. It is useful for people who have a hard time keeping track of all the ingredients in their fridge or just want some extra creativity in the kitchen.

Dude, How's My City?


This is a cost of living comparison for individuals who are looking for a new home or explore new cities. It allows users to browse cities both nationally and internationally to get average scores in many categories based on average income per profession, puvlic transport and cost of living. The is also a feature that allows the user to compare cities to eachother with the goal to find where they might find a good fit for their lifestyle.
